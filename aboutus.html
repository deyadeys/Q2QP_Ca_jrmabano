About Us

> WHO ARE WE

Angat Buhay, also known as Angat Pinas, is a charitable organization established by the 14th Vice President of the Philippines, Maria Leonor “Leni” G. Robredo. It promotes active citizenship through volunteerism and is dedicated to education, health, community engagement, and disaster response and rehabilitation. The organization's mission is to empower volunteers to uplift their fellow Filipinos and build a brighter future. It is indeed a true testament to the power of collective action and compassionate leadership. Through its tireless efforts, the organization has inspired countless individuals to take an active role in improving their communities and making a positive difference in the lives of others.

> MISSION

To empower Filipinos to become communities of active citizens as we collectively pursue a life of dignity and hope.

> VISION

Responding to the needs of marginalized communities by mobilizing the largest volunteer network in implementing Bayanihan programs.
