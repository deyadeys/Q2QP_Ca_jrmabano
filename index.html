Organization Background

Angat Buhay continues to implement community-centered and sustainable development initiatives focused on education, health and nutrition, disaster relief and rehabilitation, and community engagement and empowerment. Established by Leni Robredo, the day following the expiration of her term as Vice President of the Philippines, the organization was officially launched on July 1, 2022. The foundation's objective is to maintain the anti-poverty and pandemic response initiatives that started during Robredo's tenure as Vice President. These programs were consolidated under the name "Angat Buhay" (a Tagalog term that translates to "improve life's condition" or "lift life").

Their mission is to provide assistance to impoverished communities in the Philippines, particularly in the areas of health, education, and livelihood. Through their various programs and initiatives, Angat Buhay has been able to make a significant impact on the lives of those they serve. From building schools and providing access to clean water, to offering livelihood training and medical services, the organization has been a beacon of hope for many families. Their efforts have not gone unnoticed, as they have received recognition and support from both local and international communities. Despite the challenges they face, Angat Buhay remains committed to their cause, and will continue to work towards the betterment of the future of the Filipino people.
